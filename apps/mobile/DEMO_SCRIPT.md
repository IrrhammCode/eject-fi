# Eject.fi - Official Demo Script

This script is optimized for a 2-minute Hackathon presentation. It highlights all major judging criteria: **Solana integration, LI.FI cross-chain routing, Privy embedded wallets, and the x402 AI Monetization standard.**

---

### ⏱️ (0:00 - 0:15) THE HOOK: THE PROBLEM

**🎬 VIDEO ACTION:**
Show the initial Login Screen. The Eject.fi logo and LI.FI partner badge should be visible. *Do not click anything yet.*

**🗣️ VOICEOVER:**
> "In DeFi, protocols can be exploited in seconds. By the time you open your laptop, connect your hardware wallet, and try to withdraw or bridge your assets, your capital is already gone. Welcome to Eject.fi—an autonomous, cross-chain capital rescue protocol."

---

### ⏱️ (0:15 - 0:35) THE SOLUTION: PRIVY ONBOARDING

**🎬 VIDEO ACTION:**
Tap the **"Connect Wallet"** button. Let the Privy authentication flow (Face ID / Passkey) run smoothly until the main Dashboard appears. Scroll slightly to showcase the premium UI and live balances.

**🗣️ VOICEOVER:**
> "We built this mobile-first to ensure you can react instantly from anywhere. Using Privy Embedded Wallets, we’ve completely removed the friction of seed phrases. Users log directly into their non-custodial Solana PDA vault using simple biometric authentication."

---

### ⏱️ (0:35 - 1:00) THE AI SWARM & x402 BONUS

**🎬 VIDEO ACTION:**
Tap the **"Swarm"** action card, or type **"deep scan"** in the terminal input at the bottom and hit send. Wait for the terminal to simulate the x402 transaction process.

**🗣️ VOICEOVER:**
> "Eject.fi doesn’t rely on human reaction times. We deployed a decentralized Sentinel AI swarm that monitors Solana network health and Pyth oracles 24/7. To solve the economic alignment of decentralized AI, we implemented the Solana x402 standard. When we run a deep infrastructure scan, the client autonomously signs a micro-payment to the Agent Treasury, proving payment on-chain via the Memo program before the agent unlocks the risk report."

---

### ⏱️ (1:00 - 1:30) THE ESCAPE ROUTE: LI.FI INTEGRATION

**🎬 VIDEO ACTION:**
Tap the **"Safe Haven (Powered by LI.FI)"** card. Watch the terminal output the route comparisons and select the best bridging path.

**🗣️ VOICEOVER:**
> "Let's say the Sentinel Agent detects a critical exploit on our target protocol. Withdrawing to a compromised Solana network isn't safe enough. This is where our 'Safe Haven' feature comes in. Powered by a headless integration with the LI.FI REST API, the application instantly queries advanced routes to find the fastest bridge out of Solana—routing capital to a secure Layer 2 like Base, completely autonomously."

---

### ⏱️ (1:30 - 2:00) THE EJECTION & CLOSING

**🎬 VIDEO ACTION:**
Click the large red **"Emergency ZK-Eject"** button at the bottom. When the *Face ID / Biometric prompt* appears, authenticate. Let the terminal display the `[✓ CONFIRMED]` status.

**🗣️ VOICEOVER:**
> "When the user hits the Emergency ZK-Eject button, Eject.fi bundles the Solana Anchor withdrawal and the LI.FI bridge execution into a single, seamless flow. One biometric confirmation, and your funds are rescued, shielded, and bridged to safety.
> 
> "This is Eject.fi. Institutional-grade security, moving at the speed of code. Thank you."

---

### 💡 Recording Tips:
* **Audio Clarity:** Record your voiceover separately in a quiet room, then sync it with the screen recording using an editor like CapCut or Premiere.
* **Pacing:** Don't click the buttons too quickly. Give the judges a few seconds to read the terminal outputs (like the LI.FI routes or the x402 payment confirmation) before moving to the next action.
